About Emfound

Empowering Children Through Care, Education, and Opportunity

Emfound (Emmanuel Foundation and Outreach Development) is committed to improving the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children by providing access to care, education, and meaningful opportunities.

Through outreach programs in underserved communities, hospitals, and correctional facilities, we create lasting impact and empower children to thrive.
